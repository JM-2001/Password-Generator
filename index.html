Information about Password Security

  • Why is it important?
    • Password Security is important because it is the key to protecting your accounts from malicious actors.
  • How can you improve your password security?
    • Never use your passwords more than once. If there's a password breach in any of your accounts, the malicious actors can't use that breached password to access your other accounts.
    • Never use common passwords or easy-to-guess passwords.
    • Don't use passwords that can be guessed using information from your social media accounts.
    • Don't use words or phrases in your passwords. In addition, don't create passwords with numbers or special characters that resemble words or phrases.
    • Create complex passwords with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters to increase the strength of the password. This can be done by yourself or using password generators.
    • Use Multi-Factor Authentication(MFA) or 2-Factor Authentication(2FA).
    • Use a password manager to save and store your passwords. Although writing your passwords down isn't suggested, you can write them down BUT they should be kept in a safe location.
  • Is there a way to check if your passwords have been leaked?
    • Yes, you can use the website haveibeenpwned.com to check if your accounts have been compromised.
